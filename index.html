Installation & Sideloading

To maintain advanced features that bypass system restrictions, this application is distributed via IPA. Choose your preferred environment:

SideStore (Recommended – No PC required after setup) BEST AltStore (Reliable – Requires periodic PC sync) Sideloadly (Quick desktop-to-device installs) TrollStore (Permanent – Limited iOS versions)
Configuration Note: After installation, navigate to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management to authorize the developer profile on your device.
