Open Player – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

Open Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All media playback, document viewing, and equaliser settings operate entirely on your device.

Data Processed Locally

The following data is used by the app but never leaves your device:

  • Media files — audio, video, and document files you open are processed locally by the app's playback engine.
  • Playback preferences — EQ settings, skip amounts, playback speed, and playlist data are stored in local app storage (UserDefaults / Core Data) on your device.
  • Online stream URLs — if you add an internet stream, the app connects directly to that URL to fetch audio or video. No stream data passes through our servers.

Network Access

Open Player only makes network requests when you explicitly add or play an internet stream. The app does not make any background network calls, phone-home requests, or analytics pings.

Permissions

  • Media Library — to import files from your device's media library.
  • Files — to open documents and media from the Files app.
  • Local Network / AirPlay — to stream audio to AirPlay devices on your network.

All permissions are optional and prompted only when the relevant feature is used.

Third-Party Services

Open Player does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

Open Player is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue on the Open Player GitHub repository .
