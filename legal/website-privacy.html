Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

This policy explains what information is collected when you visit the MadnessInnsmouth website (madnessinnsmouth.github.io) and how that information is handled.

Data Collection

This website does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. No cookies are set. No personal data is collected through this website.

The web server (GitHub Pages) may retain standard access logs (IP address, timestamp, page requested) for a limited period for security and operational purposes. These logs are controlled by GitHub and are subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement .

Third-Party Links

This website contains links to external services such as GitHub. Those services have their own privacy policies which govern data collected when you visit them.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the GitHub organisation page .
