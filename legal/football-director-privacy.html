Football Director – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

Football Director is a fully offline game. It does not connect to the internet, does not collect any personal data, and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Collected

Football Director collects no personal data. The only data stored by the game is your save game data, which is written to a local file on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

No Network Access

The game does not make any network requests. It functions entirely offline.

No Telemetry

Football Director contains no analytics, crash reporters, or usage tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Football Director does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or any user). There is no data collection mechanism in the game.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue on the MadnessInnsmouth GitHub organisation .
