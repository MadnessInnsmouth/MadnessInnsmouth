Nova Voice Assistant – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

Nova Voice Assistant runs on your Windows PC and processes most tasks locally. When you use the AI features, your voice commands are sent to third-party services (Google Gemini or Ollama) using API credentials you supply yourself. MadnessInnsmouth does not collect or store any personal data.

Microphone and Voice Data

Nova uses your microphone to capture voice commands. Depending on the Speech-to-Text (STT) engine you select in Settings:

  • Google STT (online) — audio is sent to Google's Speech-to-Text API and processed remotely. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sphinx (offline) — all recognition happens locally on your device. No audio leaves your PC.
  • Whisper (offline) — all recognition happens locally on your device. No audio leaves your PC.

Voice audio is not stored by Nova. It is captured, processed for the command, and discarded immediately.

AI Processing (Google Gemini)

When you enable the Gemini AI model, text commands (converted from your voice) are sent to Google's Gemini API using your own API key. Nova does not store conversation history on any remote server. Session memory is kept in local app storage on your device only.

All Gemini API usage is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

AI Processing (Ollama)

When you select an Ollama model, all AI processing happens on your local machine. No data is sent externally.

PC Control Actions

Nova can open apps, manage files, adjust volume, and run shell commands on your behalf. These actions are carried out locally on your PC. Nova does not log or transmit information about which apps you open or which commands you run.

No Telemetry

Nova contains no analytics, crash reporters, or usage tracking. No data is sent to MadnessInnsmouth servers at any time.

Children's Privacy

Nova Voice Assistant is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue on the Nova Voice Assistant GitHub repository .
