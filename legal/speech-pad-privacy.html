Speech Pad – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

Speech Pad is a local speech-to-text writing application. All voice recognition happens on your device using the Windows Speech Recognition engine. No audio or text is transmitted to any remote server.

Microphone Access

Speech Pad requests access to your microphone in order to capture voice input for dictation. Audio is processed in real time by the Windows Speech Recognition API and immediately discarded. No audio recording is stored by Speech Pad.

Document Data

Documents you dictate or type in Speech Pad are saved locally on your device in the location you choose. Speech Pad does not upload, sync, or transmit document contents to any external service.

No Telemetry

Speech Pad contains no analytics, crash reporters, or usage tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Speech Pad is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
