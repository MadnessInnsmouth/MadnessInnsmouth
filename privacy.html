Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

This is a hobby project. I don't collect your data, I don't track you, and I don't run analytics. You can browse this site without being watched.

What Data Is Collected

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any form of user data collection. No personal information is requested, stored, or processed through this site.

Third-Party Services

This site uses Google Fonts to load the Inter typeface. When you visit a page, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to fetch the font files. This is the only external request made. Google may log that request according to their own privacy policy.

External Links

This site links to third-party tools (SideStore, AltStore, Sideloadly, TrollStore, GitHub). These sites have their own privacy policies and I have no control over their data practices. I recommend reviewing their policies before using them.

Apps & Tools

Individual apps and tools listed on this site may have their own data practices. If an app collects or stores any data, it will be documented within that app. As a general rule, I build things that work offline and keep your data on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Since this is a hobby project, updates happen when they happen.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out via GitHub.
