Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Overview

This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you visit this website or use any software published by MadnessInnsmouth, and how that information is handled.

Website Analytics

This website does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. No cookies are set. No personal data is collected through this website.

The web server may retain standard access logs (IP address, timestamp, page requested) for a limited period for security and operational purposes. These logs are not shared with third parties.

Desktop Applications

Desktop applications published on this site may collect data necessary for their functionality (for example, microphone input for the voice assistant). Each application's privacy behaviour is documented in its own README and documentation.

Unless explicitly stated in an individual app's documentation, no data is transmitted to remote servers. Processing happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

This website may link to external services such as GitHub. Those services have their own privacy policies which govern data collected when you visit them.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on the relevant GitHub repository.
