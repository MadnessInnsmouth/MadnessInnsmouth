Open Player Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Open Player is designed for on-device use. No tracking, no analytics, no ad SDKs, and no routine online data transmission.

Scope

This policy applies to Open Player, the iOS media player published by MadnessInnsmouth.

What Open Player does not collect

  • No account registration or sign-in data.
  • No tracking identifiers for advertising or profiling.
  • No analytics or telemetry events sent to the developer.
  • No crash-reporting services configured by the developer.
  • No background upload of your media library or documents.

How your files are handled

Open Player is built to work with files you choose from Files and Photos. Playback, rendering, and local processing are performed on your device. Media and document data is not viewed by the developer.

AI-assisted features (important exception)

If you choose to use AI features such as descriptions, summarisation, or analysis, the content needed to perform that request is sent directly to the selected AI provider. The developer does not receive, view, or manually inspect that content.

Data handling by the AI provider is governed by the provider's own privacy terms. You should review those terms before using AI-assisted features.

Children's privacy

Open Player is not designed to collect personal data from children. The app does not include account systems or behavioural tracking.

Changes to this policy

If Open Player privacy practices change, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

Contact
