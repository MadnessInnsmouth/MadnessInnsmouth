Football Director Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Football Director does not require account creation and does not include third-party ad tracking.

Data and gameplay information

Save files and gameplay data are stored locally on your device. The developer does not collect personal profile data through a dedicated account system.

Online play

Football Director includes online multiplayer features. Network traffic needed to establish and maintain online matches may include technical connection data.

Analytics and tracking

No advertising SDKs or behavioural ad tracking systems are integrated by the developer.

Contact

Questions about Football Director privacy? Open an issue on GitHub (opens in new tab).
