SpeechPad Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SpeechPad is built for local speech-to-text usage. No account, no tracking, and no ad analytics.

How SpeechPad processes data

SpeechPad performs transcription locally on your Windows device once the local model is installed. Audio input and transcripts are not uploaded to a MadnessInnsmouth server.

Data collection

  • No user account collection.
  • No behavioural tracking or advertising IDs.
  • No analytics configured by the developer.

Model download

An internet connection may be required initially to download speech models. Any requests involved in model download are handled by the relevant hosting provider.

Contact

Questions about SpeechPad privacy? Open an issue on GitHub (opens in new tab).
