Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

MadnessInnsmouth is committed to making all apps, tools, and content accessible to everyone — including people who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, switch controls, and voice navigation.

Accessibility is not an afterthought here. It is the foundation everything is built on.

Standards We Follow

This website and the applications listed on it aim to conform to:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines published by the W3C
  • Apple's Accessibility Best Practices — including full VoiceOver support for iOS apps

What We Do

  • Semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy on every page
  • ARIA landmarks and labels where needed for screen readers
  • Keyboard-navigable interface with visible focus indicators
  • Skip-to-content links on every page
  • Sufficient colour contrast ratios throughout
  • No content that relies solely on colour, shape, or sound
  • Responsive design that works at any zoom level and on any screen size

Known Limitations

This is a hobby project maintained by one person. While every effort is made to keep things accessible, some areas may not yet be perfect. If you find an issue, please let me know.

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility barriers on this site or in any of the apps, please reach out via GitHub. I take accessibility feedback seriously and will address it as quickly as I can.
