Apps

Applications for Windows and iOS built with accessibility as a core principle. Every app works with popular screen readers and follows platform accessibility guidelines.

Open Player

iOS

A fully VoiceOver-accessible media player and document viewer for iPhone and iPad. Plays audio, video, and streams, with a 10-band EQ, playlists, AirPlay, and a built-in document viewer for PDFs, ePubs, and more.

Open Player

Windows

An accessible media player for Windows with full screen reader support and keyboard navigation. Play audio and video files with ease.

Speech Pad

Windows

A speech-to-text writing pad for Windows. Dictate notes and documents hands-free with screen reader compatibility and a clean, accessible interface.
