Features

Built around one principle: your audio stays on your device.

🔒

100% Private

All processing runs locally on your machine. No audio is ever sent to a server. No accounts, no telemetry, no network requests during use.

🤖

On-Device AI

Powered by a downloadable local AI model that runs entirely on your hardware. Works offline once the model is installed.

⚡

Fast

No round-trips to a server. Transcription starts immediately and processes in real time — latency is limited only by your hardware.

✍️

Speech-to-Text

Dictate notes, documents, messages, or anything else. Accurate transcription for a wide range of accents and speaking styles.

📦

No Install Required

Runs as a portable application. No setup wizard, no administrator rights needed — just download and run.

🌐

Fully Offline

Once the AI model is downloaded, SpeechPad works without any internet connection.

How It Works

Local AI — no black boxes, no cloud dependencies.

  1. Download SpeechPad — a single portable .exe, no installation required.
  2. Download an AI model — on first run, SpeechPad prompts you to download a local speech recognition model. This is a one-time step.
  3. Start speaking — press record, speak, and your words appear on screen. Everything runs on your machine.
  4. Copy or save your text — export your transcript however you need it.
