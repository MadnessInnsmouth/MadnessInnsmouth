Open Player

iOS

A fully VoiceOver-accessible media player and document viewer for iPhone and iPad. Handles local files, online streams, playlists, and a wide range of document formats — all without ever needing a mouse.

iOS VoiceOver Compatible AirPlay Dynamic Type

Media Playback

Open Player handles virtually any audio or video you throw at it — local files from your library or remote streams from the internet.

  • Audio formats — MP3, FLAC, AAC, M4A, ALAC, WAV, and more
  • Video formats — MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, and more
  • Online streams — HLS playlists, HTTP MP4s, and MP3 streams
  • Custom skip amounts — jump back or forward by 5, 10, 15, 30, 60, 90, or 120 seconds, configurable to your preference
  • Playback speed — from 0.25× to 4× with no pitch distortion
  • Pitch shift — raise or lower pitch by up to 12 semitones, independently of speed
  • Shuffle and Repeat — Off, Repeat All, or Repeat One

Audio Quality

Every track goes through a professional-grade signal chain built on Apple's native audio engine.

  • 10-band equalizer — shape your sound from 32 Hz all the way to 16 kHz, with ±12 dB per band and built-in presets: Flat, Bass Boost, Treble Boost, Vocal, Classical, Jazz, Rock, Electronic, Podcast, and Custom
  • Output gain — boost quiet tracks or compensate for headphones, up to 200%
  • High-quality time-stretching — speed changes sound natural, not chipmunk-like
  • Spatial audio and Dolby Atmos passthrough — on supported devices

Playlists and Queue

  • Create and name your own playlists
  • Drag to reorder tracks inside a playlist
  • Manage the playback queue on the fly — reorder or remove tracks without stopping playback

Background Playback and System Integration

  • Background playback — audio keeps going when you lock the screen or switch apps
  • Lock Screen and Control Centre — full Now Playing card with album art, a scrubbing bar, and skip buttons
  • AirPlay — send audio to Apple TV, HomePod, or any Bluetooth or AirPlay speaker

Audio Extraction from Video

Pull the audio track out of any video file and save it as a standalone audio file.

  • Output formats: M4A (AAC), WAV (uncompressed), or ALAC (lossless)
  • Progress bar with cancel support
  • Share the result or add it straight to your library

Document Viewer

Open Player doubles as a document viewer, supporting a wide range of file types so you can read anything without a separate app.

  • PDF — native text selection, search, single-page, continuous scroll, or two-page layout, plus a night mode
  • HTML / XHTML — clean reader view with adjustable font, size, line spacing, and theme
  • Plain text and Markdown — selectable text with native rendering
  • RTF — fully rendered rich text
  • Word (DOCX) and ePub — opened through the system's Quick Look engine for accurate formatting

Accessibility

Open Player was designed from the ground up to work beautifully with VoiceOver. Every single control has a label, a hint, and a value so you always know exactly what you're touching.

  • Every button, slider, and list row is fully labelled for VoiceOver
  • Progress sliders respond to VoiceOver's swipe-up / swipe-down adjustments — no dragging required
  • Dynamic Type — all text scales with your system font-size setting
  • High-contrast colours throughout; no information is conveyed by colour alone
  • Related items are grouped cleanly so VoiceOver reads them together rather than item by item
  • A VoiceOver Tips screen in Settings walks you through all the custom gestures
