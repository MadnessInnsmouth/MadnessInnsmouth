Features

🏆

25 Leagues

England, Spain, France, Germany, and Italy — five tiers deep in each. Domestic cups and UEFA club competitions included.

📺

Match Engine

Event-by-event commentary with 16 distinct goal types. Attendance reacts to your form, division, and club reputation.

🎯

Tactics

Six formations, five mentalities, play styles, and five sliders. Win by out-thinking the opposition, not just out-spending them.

🤝

Transfers

Three transfer types with country-accurate seasonal windows. Negotiate contracts and build the squad you actually want.

🌱

Youth & Scouting

Run your academy, set training focuses, and send scouts out early. Find and develop talent before anyone else does.

🏟️

Infrastructure

Stadium, training ground, and youth facilities each rated 1–10. Invest in the club's foundations to attract better players.

🌐

Online Multiplayer

Cloud save sync and peer-to-peer play via room code. Take on real opponents managing rival clubs.

📦

Zero Install

One .exe file. No installer, no dependencies. Download and play immediately.
