Football Director

A deep football management simulation built for people who want real depth. Five nations, 25 leagues, a full match engine with commentary, transfers, youth development, scouting, infrastructure, and online multiplayer — all in a single portable executable.

Download See Features

Features

No hand-holding. Just a complete simulation of the beautiful game.

🏆

25 Leagues Across 5 Nations

England, Spain, France, Germany, and Italy — five tiers deep in each nation, plus domestic cup competitions and UEFA club tournaments.

📺

Full Match Engine

Event-by-event commentary, 16 distinct goal templates, and a realistic attendance model that reacts to your club's form and reputation.

🎯

Tactical Depth

Six formations, ATK and DEF modifiers, five mentalities, play styles, and five tactical sliders. Win by out-thinking your opponent.

🤝

Transfer Market

Three transfer types with country-accurate seasonal windows. Negotiate contracts, manage wages, and build the squad you actually want.

🌱

Youth & Development

Run your youth academy, set training focuses, and deploy scouts to find the next generation before anyone else does.

🏟️

Club Infrastructure

Invest in your stadium, training facilities, and youth setup. Infrastructure rated 1–10 affects your ability to attract players and develop talent.

🌐

Online Multiplayer

Cloud save sync and peer-to-peer multiplayer via room code. Manage rival clubs against real opponents.

📦

Zero Install

A single portable .exe — no installer, no administrator rights, no dependencies. Download and play immediately.

Tactics & Players

Enough depth to reward real decision-making.

Tactical System

  • Six formations with individual ATK and DEF modifiers
  • Five mentalities ranging up to ±28% influence on team behaviour
  • Play style selection to define how your team attacks and defends
  • Five tactical sliders for fine-grained control over team shape

Player Attributes

  • Six core attributes rated on a 1–99 scale
  • Position-weighted overall rating — the same player scores differently at different positions
  • Morale system that responds to results, playing time, and contract status
  • Contract management with wages, duration, and release clauses
